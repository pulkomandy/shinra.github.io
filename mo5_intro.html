Introduction

The Thomson MO5 is a French 8-bit computer. It's quite known there because it was part of a government plan to put computers in all schools. The educative software was usually quite bad and made most French people hate computing. It is quite a limited machine, but that's what demoing is all about : pushing the limits.

Most documentation on the machine is only available as scanned PDF of old books, and in French. The idea of this page is to sum up all of it in an accessible format, using hyperlinks, and all that modern webstuff.
